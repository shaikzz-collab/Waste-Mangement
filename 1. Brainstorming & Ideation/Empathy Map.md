## Key Insights for App Design

1. **Keep it Instant**: Alex has less than 10 seconds of attention span when standing in front of trash bins. The search bar must load and respond instantly.
2. **Actionable Steps**: Alex needs numbered instructions (e.g., "Remove the foil lid", "Rinse clean") rather than just a simple "Yes/No" decision.
3. **Geographical Navigation**: Alex does not know where to drop off hazardous materials. The application must show a clear, filterable map of local centers with routing links.
